#### merge

#### pull
<!-- Your defnition here -->

#### push
<!-- Your defnition here -->

#### reset
<!-- Your defnition here -->
Resets the changes you've made in the current repo to your last commit.

#### rm
<!-- Your defnition here -->

#### status
